Grilled Bacon Jalapeño Wraps

Description

Delicious grilled jalapeño peppers stuffed with cream cheese. Easy to make on the grill!

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
  • 6 fresh jalapeno poppers, halved lengthwise and seeded
  • 12 slices of bacon
  • 12 toothpicks, soaked in water (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the gate
  2. Spread cream cheese in each jalapeno half; wrap with bacon. Secure with a toothpick if needed
  3. Cook on the preheated grill until bacon is crispy, turning often, about 10 minutes.
